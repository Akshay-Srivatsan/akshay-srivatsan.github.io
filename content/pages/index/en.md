# Akshay Srivatsan

## About Me

![An image of me][portrait]

I'm a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Stanford University,
advised by Professors [Keith Winstein][keithw] and [Dawson Engler][engler] and
working in the Systems and Networking Research Group at Stanford. My main
research topic is applying functional programming principles and ideas to
(distributed) operating systems.

In addition to my Ph.D. advisors, I've also had the privilege of being advised
by Professors [Frans Kaashoek][kaashoek] and [Sara Achour][sachour] on
different projects.
